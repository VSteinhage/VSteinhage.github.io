Volker Steinhage: News



Oct. 10, 2019: The German Ministry of Education and Research (BMBF) grants funding of AMMOD, a research consortium for constructing Automated Multisensor Stations for Monitoring of BiodDiversity for three years.



Sep. 23, 2019: Team member Jenny Mack defended her PhD thesis successfully. Congrats!



Nov. 29/30, 2018: Participation of Volker Steinhage at the Workshop on Field Phenotyping in Breeding organized by the Federal Ministry of Food and Agriculture (BMLE), the German Federation for Plant Innovation (GFPi) and the Research Center Jülich (FZ Jülich).



Feb. 9, 2017: For his teaching of the module Intelligent Vision Systems in the Bachelor programme Volker Steinhage receives a teaching award from the faculty.

Volker Steinhage wishes to share the award with all the excellent student tutors who conducted the exercises of the module in the last years: Jenny Balfer, Jenny Mack, Christian Lenz, Jonatan Grimm, Mark Hoffman.
